How it works

Here's a Hand makes it easier for family and friends to offer a hand.

Family and friends can set up an online group to quickly and easily send messages by mobile phone text and email.

Each person can decide what they want to offer, and when and how they'd like to be contacted.

So people will only receive a message if the request matches what they have offered.

Mary has a problem...her lightbulb has blown!

Mary’s son, Jake, would normally help out but he is at work. He sends a message to Here’s A Hand via SMS

Mary’s neighbour, Anna, spots the message in her inbox. She pops round & fixes the light bulb in no time!

Thank goodness for Here’s A Hand!

There is a welcome guide you can download and pass to others who are interested.
